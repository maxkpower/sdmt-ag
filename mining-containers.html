Minerbase020

Designed by miners for miners. The Minerbase020 is a 20FT ISO Container with CSC & Sea Freight approval. All containers are hand build in the Netherlands and can be fully customized according to your wishes.

  • Max 500 KW of raw air-cooled hashing power
  • Houses up to 176 Antminer S17s
Cooling

This allows users to modify materials, lighting and render "on the fly"

  • Hot-aisle cold-aisle setup with passive cooling (or active cooling where required)
  • Passive cooling: Aluminium industrial-grade louvers on both long sides
  • Active cooling: Aluminium industrial-grade louvers on intake side, industrial-grade high CFM exhaust fans on outtake side
  • Backplates corresponding to miner type, to maintain a closed border between hot-aisle and cot-aisle
  • Filtered fresh air trough industrial grade filters with maximum airflow capacity of 100.000 m3/h
  • Airflow pressure sensors to analyze and determine replacement time of the filters
